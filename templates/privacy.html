Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 13, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data Spendly collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — the transactions, amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you choose to enter.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as page visits and error reports, used solely to keep the service running smoothly.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain your Spendly account.
  • Display your expense history and summaries.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve reliability.
  • Send essential service communications (e.g., password resets).

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service for you.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secured servers with access controls and encrypted connections (HTTPS). We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

While we take reasonable precautions, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your Spendly account.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own purposes. We may use a limited set of trusted service providers (such as a hosting provider) to operate the service — these providers are contractually bound to handle your data only as directed by us.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms that track you across sites, or any external financial services.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — update inaccurate information through your account settings.
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data at any time.
  • Portability — request an export of your expense data in a machine-readable format.

6. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party cookies of any kind.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. For significant changes, we will notify you via the email address on your account.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise any of your rights, please reach out via the contact information provided in your account settings. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
